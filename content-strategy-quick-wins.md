## Content Creation Strategy

For each article:
1. **Start with the question as the H1**
2. **Include a "Part of [Pillar]" callout** at the top
3. **Answer directly** in the first paragraph
4. **Use real examples** from your projects where possible
5. **Include actionable frameworks** or step-by-step guides
6. **Add "Related Resources" section** at the end with 3-4 internal links
7. **Target 800-1200 words** for optimal SEO value
8. **Include 2-3 images/diagrams** if relevant
