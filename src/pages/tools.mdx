## Why These Tools?

Our stack is built around three core principles:

### **üöÄ Speed of Execution**
Every tool must accelerate our time from idea to testable prototype. If it takes longer to set up than to validate, we choose something else.

### **üîß Modularity**
Components should be swappable without breaking the entire system. This lets us experiment with new approaches without rewriting everything.

### **üë§ One-Person Operations**
We optimize for tools that don't require large teams or complex coordination. AI augments what one person can accomplish.
