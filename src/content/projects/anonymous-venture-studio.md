# The Unnamed Road

**One founder. Seven sites. AI agents doing the work.**

The Unnamed Road is a single-operator AI-native venture studio. AI agents handle keyword research, article generation, publishing, and analytics – all self-hosted on Coolify and Hetzner for around €35 per month.

## The Thesis

The traditional startup model – large teams, venture capital, years of planning – is no longer the only path. One person paired with AI can now do what previously required 8–12 people.

Mission: "Engineer reality faster than bureaucracy can react."

## How It Runs

- **23 active n8n workflows** – keyword research, content generation, publishing, analytics
- **100% AI-published content** across all ventures
- **Self-hosted on Coolify + Hetzner** – replaces €200+/month in managed SaaS
- **Concept to live site in 48 hours**
- **Zero manual content creation**

## The Stack

Coolify · Hetzner · n8n · OpenClaw · Umami · Minio · Groq · Gemini

## Active Ventures

- **The Hockey Analytics** – proactive NHL data research, 499 SEK/mån premium
- **The Hockey Brain** – analytics consulting for hockey clubs and national teams
- **The Agent Fabric** – distributed agent infrastructure for enterprise
- **The Atomic Network** – hyperlocal GTM via physical mail + referral loops
- **The Print Route** – print order routing from webhook to doorstep
- **The Data Labelers** – validated training data with EU AI Act compliance
- **Emil Ingemark Karlsson** – data engineering and full-stack consulting

## Operating Principle

Build without permission. Ship without a team. Let the data decide what survives.
